Ways to stay connected
Onboard
Well hello there...

New things can be confusing, but that can be turned around with good communication. Keep new customers excited and coming back for more with tips and recommendations.

Notify
All news is good news

Well-informed customers are happier customers. Send them updates about new products, new styles, new features, or just plain news.

Upsell
Act now

Give a gentle nudge to customers with new offers related to their past interests.

Nurture
Let’s keep this going

Customer relationships are forever—we hope. To increase your chances of a long-term relationship, keep them engaged with suggestions, best practices, and interesting info.

Mitigate
Heads up!

Everyone appreciates an early warning. Proactively let customers know about service updates or on-going issues to prevent frustration.
