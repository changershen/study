Editorial Calendar

Editorial Calendar for Planning, Producing, and Publishing Help Center Posts

(0)
Install

Free

The Editorial Calendar from Marketing.AI helps you to organize your whole content team, providing them with all the tools they need on one single guided track. It enables you to easily build a shared Help Center content calendar, to define personas, to manage your themes, to collaborate with your colleagues, and to understand what is working, and what isn't.

Since it is part of the Marketing.AI Content Marketing Framework, you can tie together your messaging for supporting customers and helping them succeed, with the content you used to acquire them in the first place.

Use the Editorial Calendar App to create and manage more content for your Help Center, and make it more relevant to your customers.

  1. If you already have a Marketing.AI account, login to your dashboard. If not, sign-up for a free trial of the Marketing.AI Content Marketing Software Framework, including Editorial Calendar Software.
  2. Navigate to Settings -> Integrations -> Zendesk.
  3. Input your Zendesk subdomain (ie if your Zendesk url is https://sample_subdomain.zendesk.com, then just enter sample_subdomain).
  4. Click Integrate Zendesk Account and follow the instructions to authorize your account.
  5. Plan and create your first post in the Calendar and Draft Editor. To publish to your Help Center, select the section you want to publish to from the Publishing Targets drop-down list.
