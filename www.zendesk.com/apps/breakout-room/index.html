Breakout Room

Demo of ticket, user and account APIs

(8)
Install

Free

With Breakout Room, your users and agents can create and share screen recordings.

When submitting a help request, users can seamlessly record their screen and add recordings to tickets.

Agents can view submitted recordings, create new recordings and even include previously-recorded screencasts in their replies.

Breakout Room embeds in your Zendesk request pages and Help Center. There’s nothing for your users or agents to download.

All recordings are hosted in the cloud. Build a library of content and re-use them in your knowledge base, website or anywhere else.

Resolve tickets faster by letting your customers and agents communicate visually. See the exact issue users have and show them exactly what to do next.

**New: Screen recording for iOS apps is now available. Replay how users interact with your mobile apps and instantly replicate issues such as crashes and bugs.

Adding Breakout Room to Zendesk

The Breakout Room app for Zendesk app lets your Zendesk agents view, create and share screen recordings with customers and other agents. To get started, you’ll need an active Breakout Room account. Just signup on our website! No worries, you’ll get a free trial.

Now, let’s go through a few steps to finish your setup.

  1. First, create a new custom ticket field. Make sure to call this “Screen Recording URL.”
    a. The "Visible" field should be checked.
    b. The "Editable" field should be checked.
  2. Save the new field and copy the “Custom Field ID.” You’ll need that in a moment.
  3. Next, install the Breakout Room app from the Zendesk marketplace.
  4. In the app settings for Breakout Room, add your Breakout Room Identifier key. This is found under Settings -> Screen Recording -> Breakout Room Zendesk Application Token
  5. For the Breakout Room Custom Ticket Field ID, use Custom field ID for the “Screen Recording URL” that you created in Step 1.
    a. Navigate to Ticket Fields
    b. For the “Screen Recording URL” field, click “Edit”
    c. The Custom field ID will be displayed at the top of the page

Click “Install” to finish the app installation!

Embedding Screen Recording in New Request Pages

Next, to let customers submit screen recordings, just add the Breakout Room javascript to your “New Request Page.”

  1. Navigate to “Customize Design” in Zendesk’s knowledge base
  2. Click “Edit Theme”
  3. Edit your “New Request” template
  4. Add your Breakout Room javascript to the very bottom of the template page
    a. Your javascript can be found in Breakout Room under Settings->Screen Recording
  5. Next, navigate to the “Agent View” in Zendesk.
  6. Create a custom Ticket Field entitled “Screen Recording URL”
    a. The "Visible" field should be checked.
    b. The "Editable" field should be checked.
