Zendesk Partners

Join us

Become a Zendesk partner to help companies provide a great overall customer experience and build relationships that are more meaningful, personal, and productive.
JOIN

Zendesk makes software for improving the relationships between companies and their customers. As a Zendesk Partner, you will connect to a new set of opportunities, take advantage of resources that will help you build your expertise, and grow your business.

Explore our Partner Programs

  • Referral Partner

    Consultants, integrators, and IT professionals focused on the customer support space, helping large enterprises be human at scale

    Learn more

  • Solution Provider

    Customer engagement experts that resell Zendesk, manage complex implementations, design custom experiences, and offer an array of other services

    Learn more

  • Business Service Provider

    Outsourcers and Business Process as a Service providers that leverage Zendesk to offer innovative customer engagement solutions

    Learn more
