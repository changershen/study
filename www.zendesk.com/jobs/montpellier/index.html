    • Product Designer

      As a product design team, we believe in a collaborative work style with a bias towards rapid prototyping, experimentation, and a strong curiosity for customers needs.


      What we’re looking for:

      We’re looking for a product designer whose portfolio demonstrates excellence in visual design and a capacity to solve complex problems with simple, empathetic solutions. As a product designer at Zendesk, you will be an important part of our global creative team which spans the disciplines of product design, user research, brand, and storytelling.


      What you get to do everyday:

      • Act as a conduit between the Product and Creative teams to solve complex interaction problems, humanize product ideas, and develop these ideas into elegant application design
      • Work with Product Managers and Engineers to grow concepts through design exploration, ideation and user validation
      • Design interaction models, wireframes and mid-fidelity mockups that promote ease of use and optimize how customers interact with our products
      • Analyze tasks, model information, navigation, and flows using UX best practices for the web
      • Present design solutions to stakeholders and company executives, defend design decisions and incorporate feedback into the design cycle
      • Strike a balance between the practicalities of design and rethinking paradigms that define a new web
      • Ideate with all levels of Zendesk to create products that make happier support professionals

      What you bring to the role:

      • Bachelor’s degree (or similar) and a minimum of 3+ years of professional experience designing consumer or SaaS applications
      • A demonstrated track record of shipping products
      • A love for data visualizations and a passion for telling stories with information
      • Strong command of user research, interaction design, visual design, and information architecture
      • Capable of communicating interactions and prototyping rapidly at all levels of resolution
      • Demonstrated use of data as a means to guide iterations and to measure design success
      • A love for validating designs and deploying various forms of user testing throughout your process, both formal and informal
      • A strong online portfolio that showcases your process, provides detail of your role on projects and shows real examples of functional designs that you have developed from start to finish

      Zendesk builds software for better customer relationships. It empowers organizations to improve customer engagement and better understand their customers. More than 80,000 paid customer accounts in 150 countries and territories use Zendesk products. Based in San Francisco, Zendesk has operations in the United States, Europe, Asia, Australia, and South America. Learn more at www.zendesk.com.

    • Operations Engineer

      Zendesk is a rapidly growing company with an amazing product and exceptional employees. We’re looking for a dynamic DevOps Engineer to join our growing Devops team in Montpelllier, France!

      As part of the Devops team at Zendesk you’ll solve problems relating to mission critical services and build automation to prevent problem recurrence.  Our mission is to accelerate product engineering productivity without sacrificing reliability.

      What you get to do everyday:

      • Solve complex problems and provide simple elegant solutions
      • Build and improve the tools we use to run an Alexa top 400 website with over 75,000 customers
      • Participate in engineering sprints along with the Engineering teams
      • Collaborate with your Operations teammates in Copenhagen, Dublin, Melbourne, and San Francisco
      • Improve our configuration management tooling and infrastructure
      • Design and enhance deployment pipelines for releasing new code and configuration into production
      • Contribute to making our infrastructure simple, reliable, and easy to operate
      • Analyze performance, usage patterns and capacity constraints and tune our underlying systems
      • Use a wide variety of technologies, wear lots of hats (actual hats optional)
      • Conduct periodic on-call duties using a follow the sun model
      • Work in an agile development environment and believe that pragmatism always triumphs over dogmatism

      About you:

      • BS degree in Computer Science or related technical field, or equivalent practical experience
      • Strong coding skills in at least one programming language, and a desire to pick up more (we work primarily in Ruby, Python and golang)
      • Have solid understanding and experience of building world class production services in cloud provider (AWS and Google Cloud) and colocation platforms
      • Expertise in designing, analyzing and troubleshooting large-scale distributed systems
      • Systematic problem solving approach, coupled with a strong sense of ownership and drive
      • In-depth knowledge of at least one configuration management tool
      • Experience with one or more of the following datastores (MySQL, Memcached, MongoDB, Hadoop, RIAK)
      • Extensive experience working with Unix/Linux systems and a deep knowledge of all layers of the networking stack

      • Information Technology

    • Service Desk Specialist

      We are looking for a Global Service Desk Specialist who is a sharp go-getter to act as the face for all things IT as we continue to grow. This is a brand new, autonomous role in our Service Desk team in the Montpellier office. You’ll join at an exciting time where you’ll help scale our internal IT infrastructure as we rapidly expand. You’ll support 750+ local users as well as global users remotely.


      Zendesk is a global SaaS company and our products help businesses have meaningful and engaging interactions with their customers.


      As an IT Service Desk Specialist, you should be a Mac expert, a puzzle solver, be warm, caring, funny, honest, observant, humble, rational and above all else self-aware. To be successful here you should not be shy and be ready to take on any task large or small. Day to day responsibilities can include setting up a new laptops, troubleshooting complex configuration issues or training users.


      As an IT Service Desk Specialist there’s the opportunity to use the latest technologies, implement improvements and take on project work as we scale. All this whilst being part of a forward thinking, highly successful IT team that’s highly regarded within the business.


      What you get to do every day as an IT Service Desk Specialist:


      • As ambassador for IT to our staff at Zendesk you will continue to build the brand of ‘Best in class IT’ while delivering incredible customer service
      • With a mindfulness for quality deliverables, align with IT and Facilities resources to execute on requests in a timely fashion
      • Communicate and document system and process changes to the business. Translate system functionality into business relevant language
      • Ensuring the smooth running of all IT systems, including many cloud-based applications and local hardware policies
      • Help provision and de-provision hardware and software including cloud-based applications; licenses and asset life cycle management
      • Communicate effectively with both skilled and unskilled Mac users (and a very small group of Windows VM / PC users)
      • Maintain currency, expertise and competency of relevant products, policies, and methods for support delivery
      • Maintain a sense of humor while working in a fast-paced dynamic team

      What you bring to the role:

      • Experience supporting Apple Macs, although candidates with a Windows background will be considered
      • Previous experience in Helpdesk Support, Desktop Support, IT Support, IT Service Desk support etc
      • Experience supporting internal customers is beneficial
      • Experience with Casper, Sophos and VMs
      • Experience with Cloud tools (such as Slack, Google Apps, Zoom, etc)
      • Willingness to experiment with new equipment and new ideas
      • Passionate about Apple products and curious about learning how to manage those in an enterprise environment
      • Strong team player who understands that customer service comes first before anything else
      • Natural problem solver who enjoys identifying ways to make things better
      • Flexible and open to changing priorities and managing multiple tasks simultaneously within compressed time frames
      • Thoughtful decision making skills with a knack for identifying, prioritizing, and articulating the highest impact initiatives
      • Able to meet deliverables and drive your work to completion within specified timelines
      • Deliver ideas and solutions to many audiences including your team and IT leadership
      • Can work cross functionally in many areas without being constrained by your job function
      • Collaborative, upbeat work ethic where you can take ownership and have fun
      A fluency in both French and English is required 

      Culture and Perks

      We have 50+ Staff in our Montpellier office which is home to our analytics product - BIME. We approach work with calm, focus, a sense of humour whilst keeping sane working hours. At Zendesk we also have a culture where ideas come from the bottom up - we have yearly company hackathon!

      Perks include stocks, flexible working hours, training allowance, medical cover, mobile phone allowance, and generous paid time off.
