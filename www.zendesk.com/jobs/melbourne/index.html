      Responsibilities:

        • Answer all questions related to Zendesk and escalate when necessary
        • Educate and empower our customers to become better Zendesk users
        • Own the customer experience and work to exceed their expectations. We want to treat our customers as if you would treat a guest in your own home.
        • Proactively look for solutions to problems and propose improvements if something could work better
        • Be Zendesk’s ambassador for all internal departments and help them be successful in their roles
        • Drive product change and improvement to make Zendesk the leading support platform

      Experience

      Soft Skills:

        • You love talking to people on the phone and building relationships with your customers

        • You’re able to make decisions and solve problems
        • You have an ability to explain complex issues in beautifully simple terms
        • You’re curious and have a natural ability to “zoom out” of a problem, in order to ask the right questions.
        • You’re able to plan, organize, and prioritize work - this role wears many hats!
      Technical skills and Experience required:
        • At least 1 year experience in Customer Service
        • Ability to evaluate, troubleshoot, and follow-up on customer issues as well as replicate and document for further escalation
        • Experience using social media or web-based software, including Twitter, Facebook, Google Apps is an advantage
        • Knowledge of common help desk/ticketing solutions is a plus
        • Knowledge of HTML, XML, JavaScript or CSS   is a plus
        • Fluent in Japanese and in English

    • Director of Product, Platform

      As Director of Product for Platform, you will be running a global team of driven and empowered product managers, overseeing the priority of work for multiple development teams, all while working with our customers, partners, and internal developers to produce a strategic vision for our developer platform and developer experience.

      You will be primarily responsible for our developer platform, which includes our multi-product APIs, SDKs, Web Widgets, apps frameworks, and more. In addition, you will lead product for our developer experience (developer portal, documentation, and partner tools) and strategic integrations with products like Salesforce, JIRA, and Shopify. You will exhibit exceptional skills in problem extraction and relationship building when working with customers, partners, and developers.

      You must understand, through experience, what it is to be a developer using an open platform, and apply this knowledge and empathy to the overall vision and immediate roadmap that you own. You must also understand and have experience with managing a global team across numerous offices and timezones.

      What you will be doing:

      • Lead our product development for our APIs, SDKs, apps frameworks, strategic integrations, and developer experience products, continuing to expand our platform to enable developers, customers, and partners looking to integrate, extend, or customize Zendesk
      • Create a strategy for integration of Zendesk’s acquisitions by identifying what shared services may be required and planning how those acquisitions absorb those services
      • Managing a team of Product Managers for our shared services, working closely with engineering leadership to make clear the vision for Zendesk’s shared services, setting goals and milestones to achieve that vision
      • Partner closely with our product marketing, sales, and customer success teams to bring new service features to market and drive adoption within our developer community
      • Distill Zendesk’s competitive positioning, customer research, and new technology developments into a clear platform strategy that will differentiate our services in the mid-market and enterprise space
      What you bring to the role:
      • Minimum 5 years of experience in product management within high paced, agile software-as-a-service environments, preferably in the B2B enterprise space; including daily interactions with designers and software developers
      • Minimum 3 years of experience creating platform for developers, including REST APIs, SDKs, and frameworks
      • Deep understanding of a developer platform in a B2B software context
      • Innately passionate about developer experience and new technologies, with a platform-first mentality; you have working knowledge of web architecture, service and API design and stay current with development trends
      • Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the willingness to present and defend your ideas to other key stakeholders, including partners, and customers
      • Proven product leadership, preferably of global teams, and practical development skills.

      • Improve operability, maintainability, stability and performance of underlying Zendesk systems

      • Test and automate everything!
