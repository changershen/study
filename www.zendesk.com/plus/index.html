常见问题及回答

  • 试用

  • 免费试用是如何工作?

    +
    -

    When you sign up for your trial of Support you'll have access to all Professional features. If you want to trial a specific plan, just contact us. At any point during the trial you can choose a plan and pay by credit card from within your account.

  • 我可以体验支持,一起聊天和说话吗?

    +
    -

    Yes! Chat will automatically be included in your Support trial. You can choose to add Talk to your trial as well.

  • 在试验结束后会发生什么?

    +
    -

    At the end of a Support trial, your data and setup remains intact. You can login and select a plan to purchase.

  • 购买

  • 我怎么购买软件?

    +
    -

    Just sign up for a trial and within your trial account you can purchase today. You can pay by credit card online, or our top level plans include an invoicing option.

  • 你的合同是多久?我可以改变计划吗?

    +
    -

    Plans are month-to-month or annual. You can start/stop or make changes to your plan at any time (but note that refunds are not given for cancellation or downgrades). Contact us for details.

  • 我可以按买功能购买吗?有什么附加费用吗?

    +
    -

    With Add-ons for our Support product, you can pick and choose additional features that you need in addition to your core plan. Contact us if you would like to purchase an Add-on.

  • 有捆绑价格支持,聊天和说话?

    +
    -

    The Lite plan of Chat comes free with Support. You can upgrade to Chat's Basic, Advanced or Premium plan on any Support plan. You can add Talk to any Support plan.

  • 有什么折扣吗?

    +
    -

    We offer a discounted price for subscriptions billed annually. The prices shown on this page reflect this annual discount.

  • 一般问题

  • 什么是代理?

    +
    -

    An agent is what we call anyone who logs into a Zendesk product. Each rep, admin, or manager needs their own agent license. Add as many agents as you want on any plan.

  • endesk产品可以与其他应用程序和系统集成吗?

    +
    -

    We have hundreds of pre-built apps and integrations. But you can build almost anything you want on top of the Zendesk platform via our developer API.

  • 我的数据是私有的吗?安全怎么样?

    +
    -

    Yes, we combine enterprise-class security features with comprehensive audits of our applications, systems, and networks to ensure customer and business data is always protected. Take a look at the security measures we take to protect your business and your customers.

  • 更多问题?

    +
    -

    Our Help Center is open 24/7 or reach out to our global support team. We're here to help.
