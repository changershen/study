Lucidchart

Charting a path to 99% customer satisfaction

Challenge

Lucidchart is a web-based diagramming application used to create visual communications right in a web browser. With hundreds of thousands of users, ranging from individuals to SMBs to enterprise businesses, its support needs can be extensive. At first, Lucidchart handled its support with four separate email accounts. It was impossible to tell which member of our team was logged into an account, says Brett Bowman, Head of Customer Strategy. We were regularly yelling out, Im looking at the email about such-and-such; no one else is in it, right? Bowman knew this system wasnt sustainable. Using email definitely wasnt scalable from a customer, onboarding, or training perspective. Bowmans goal for Lucidchart was to transform its customer support process and he started looking for customer service software solutions. First, he wanted a tool to help handle Lucidcharts most common questions, which typically involve browser-related issues. Next, he wanted to increase Lucidcharts ability to direct users toward and encourage them to use self-service. Also, because Lucidchart utilizes a freemium business model, product packages vary in features, functionality, and number of licenses. The result is a product with high sales volumes at relatively lower price points (including zero). To handle all this complexity, Lucidchart needed to find a solution that offered straightforward, efficient, and customizable functionality as well as a system that would empower users to take advantage of Lucidcharts full knowledge base through an intuitive support portal.

Zendesk as a solution

We considered another solution along with Zendesk in our search for a new tool, explains Mitch Dumke, Manager of User Experience, and I tested both for months. We discovered that Zendesk had a much more robust toolset that would easily scale along with our business. The other was just not dynamic enough. Dumke adds that Zendesks number of customers helped him decide it was the right choice for Lucidchart. “Zendesk has become the authoritative figure for SaaS-based support solutions,” he says. After implementing Zendesk, Lucidchart got started right away by forwarding all of its email into Zendesk. Next, they got everyone involved.

Although we have a dedicated support staff, says Bowman, everyone in our organization uses Zendesk, including our sales team and our VPs of Operations and Sales and Marketing. Now, the majority of Lucidcharts high volume sales inquiries are channeled into Zendesk and managed as a sales ticket by agents. We found this solution to be an excellent fit for our business model, says Bowman. Our Zendesk agents serve not only as customer support experts, but also as our inside sales reps. Lucidchart now uses Zendesk to flag sales tickets appropriately in order to track and measure them correctly, and team members love the transparency Zendesk provides the organization.

Lucidchart has dramatically decreased the time required to onboard new agents, in part because Zendesk enables common questions to be answered with macros. Its a night and day difference, says Bowman. Our new agents can easily view forums and email conversations of more tenured agents with related discussions, says Bowman. This allows them to learn from each other and share best practices. Within a week of transitioning to Zendesk from their email support system, Lucidchart decreased its average handling time by more than 30%. Adds Bowman, And we werent even utilizing all of the macros, triggers, automations, and other great features in Zendesk yet!

Lucidchart provides outstanding customer service with several Zendesk features:

  • Zendesks iPhone and iPad apps allow Brett to check in on the ticket queue when hes on the road, helping him escalate any urgent bugs or customer issues that need to be resolved as quickly as possible.
  • Bretts able to measure various trends, such as ticket creation, customer satisfaction, and response time by using Advanced Analytics, which enable him to stay ahead of his customers needs by forecasting ticket flow and staffing accordingly.

Results

With Zendesk, Lucidchart has seen the following results:

  • First response time: 3.3 hours within business hours; 10.8 hours overall
  • Reduction in ticket handling time: 30%
  • Customer Satisfaction Rating: 99%

Everyone in our organization uses Zendesk, from Support, to Sales, to Operations. We even copy our developers on tickets that involve bugs to give them more context. Not only does it help them smash bugs faster, but it also helps us keep track of customer concerns; we can proactively reach out to them as soon as the issue is resolved, or add another feature theyve requested.
