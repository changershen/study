ReverbNation

Customer service outgrows email

Challenge

ReverbNation provides over 2.8 million music industry professionals – artists, managers, labels, venues, festivals/events – with powerful, easy-to-use technology to promote and prosper online. Their goal is to offer the best tools for musicians and the best music for everyone else. They consider their clients small businesses, and want to help them get off the ground and be successful by supplying them a range of services, from free to paid.

Before doubling in size, ReverbNation was able to manage their customer support matters with email alone. Email worked ok when we were small and only had a few of us in support, says Neal Moody, Head of Customer Support. But as we grew, it was clear we needed something to help. Still, after adding more customer service agents, ReverbNation continued with just email. However, even though I could set up filters, it wasnt as efficient as we would have liked, says Moody. We couldnt find any deeper information, like trends or common themes. ReverbNation also wanted the ability to track its data, such as first response and resolution times. According to Moody, before ReverbNation found a customer service software solution to help, this information was challenging to nail down.

Zendesk as a solution

ReverbNation wanted a customer service solution to help with its high volume of basic questions. We wanted the ability to assign tickets easily, says Moody. We compared Zendesk with some other tools out there, but found them complicated  I wasnt impressed. Zendesk was just easier to use. ReverbNation was up and running with Zendesk in just a few hours. It was actually easier than wed made it out to be, says Moody. We directed our email to Zendesk immediately. The initial setup was simple, and creating macros, new tags, and reports took a little more time to adjust and tweak.

Using Zendesk, ReverbNation was also able to utilize key metrics to make data-driven decisions about future goals and strategies. Moody and his team now use Zendesk to track first response time, ticket volume, and much more. Zendesk has been incredibly helpful. For example, we can now see that just one change in the product can mean our ticket volume doubles. We can see everything, and these tangible metrics help us create charts and ways to present the data to help not just our team make decisions, but also our entire organization. In addition, Moody says the biggest impact ReverbNation noticed after implementing Zendesk was efficiency. Now our response time is even better.

Heres more about how ReverbNation uses Zendesk to provide superior customer service:

  • Macros allow ReverbNation to answer frequently asked questions more efficiently. Moody can quickly create a macro when theres a known bug and within minutes, everyone has the predefined response right at their fingertips.
  • Moodys team uses tags to categorize tickets, tagging them about specific bugs or product changes.
  • Zendesks advanced analytics enable Moody to create reports based on these tags. The reports show him where the team is currently, as well as where theyve been. They also provide firm data points he can share with his Product team.

Results

With Zendesk, ReverbNation has seen the following results:

  • Customer Satisfaction Rating: 91%
  • First response time: Less than 1 business day (65% lower than the industry benchmark)



Success isnt always measured in response time, but also in how effectively our team can talk to the Product team about our customers response to our products and features. Being able to put a metric behind that is critical. Our motto at ReverbNation is Artists First and Zendesk helps us do just that.
