Customer Stories

Out of Milk

Topic suggestions reduce ticket volume by 70%

Challenge

The journey to creating a successful mobile application can be a long and arduous one. Out Of Milk has experienced this first hand – their team has produced the quintessential home-life app. Their name illuminates their purpose: Out of Milk tracks your shopping needs and your pantrys inventory, and manages your to-do list items. Thanks to Out of Milk, users never have to come home from shopping just to realize they forgot to buy lemon juice, eggs, or baking soda. Over the course of a year, they have already had 3.8 million downloads – that translates to many end users with a large volume of support inquiries. With such high ticket volume there was only one thing left to do: hire more support agents. Or did they have another option?

What co-founder, developer, and support manager, Chase Sheaffer, needed was a customer support solution that allowed their team to respond instantly and effectively without having to hire extra support agents.

Zendesk as a solution

Since mobile applications are all about convenience and speed, their reply times have to be agile and quick. When Out of Milk found Zendesk, they were finally able to implement topic suggestions, which in turn lowered their ticket influx by 70%. Out of Milk was then able to continue supporting their vast customer base with just their original three agents. At one point, Out of Milk wanted to try something new and switched to a different customer support solution, but once they did, they realized you dont know what you have until its gone. Turns out, the solution they had switched to was missing some critical tools: their agent collision was unreliable, there were no topic suggestions to decrease ticket volume, and their reporting was lacking. To make matters worse, the solution also changed their name and branding overnight without warning. Immediately, Out of Milk switched back to Zendesk and fell even more in love with it than ever before…looks like absence really does make the heart grow fonder!

Out of Milk delivers a premium level of service to their end users using Zendesks:

  • Agent collision detection: With 3 agents replying to tickets as quickly as possible, knowing if a ticket is open enables the members of the Out of Milk team to focus their efforts on tickets that have not been addressed yet.
  • Search Analytics: Chase only needs to spend minutes each week analyzing his end users search queries, and creating new content to address these questions. Creating just one article can mean a 15 ticket reduction in volume for the next day, freeing him up to answer more complex questions.
  • Zendesk for iPad: In the world of mobile apps, a delay in first reply to support inquiries can mean a negative review in the app market. The Out of Milk team uses the iPad app to delight their customers with fast response times by answering tickets on the fly, even while waiting for a table at a restaurant.

Results

Here is what Out of Milk is able to achieve using Zendesk:

  • First response time: 2-5 minutes
  • Amount of tickets solved via topic suggestions: 70%
  • Average resolution time: less than 2 hours

Our goal is to increase adoption and usage of our app by providing a good app and a good customer support experience. Speed is king, and Zendesk is the key to our customer support success. With it, I can log in, read a ticket, reply and solve it, all within just a matter of minutes – Co-founder Chase Sheaffer.
