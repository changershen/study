Customer Stories

QuiBids

Rapid response is key for a fast-paced business

Challenge

You snooze, you lose. With live auctions, response time is everything. QuiBids is well aware  they are an internet auction site aimed at providing the cheapest prices for in demand items. They also include an entertainment component: games with earning potential to use for future bids. By providing such amazing deals, especially during the peak consumer season during the holidays, their auctions are in high demand and hectic. Auctions happen in real time, so tickets are generated at a high volume continuously. Consumers expect response times at light speed.

What Director of Customer Support, Josh Walker, needed was a customer support solution that allowed their agents to respond at supersonic speed so their customers wouldnt miss out on a fleeting deal.

Zendesk as a solution

QuiBids recently solved their one millionth ticket, and knows just how crazy the bidding can get when their consumers start to deck the halls. During the last holiday season, QuiBids ticket number spiked from the usual 700 per day to 1,500 tickets. Thanks to macros (predefined answers to frequently asked questions), they were able to answer common FAQs about their business model instantly and spend more time on the tickets that required special attention.

QuiBids delivers a premium level of service to their end users using Zendesks:

  • Macros: Macros are the key to QuiBids efficiency, and allow them to quickly answer frequently asked questions – and resolve their customers inquiries faster.
  • Triggers: QuiBids automatically tags each inquiry as it comes in, and then runs a trigger to automatically assign each ticket to the right team, saving them hours they would otherwise spend assigning tickets manually.
  • Advanced Analytics: Zendesks powerful Advanced Analytics help QuiBids keep an eye on the health of their customer support organization. They review ticket trends daily to see if they are hitting their 24-hour response time goal, discover which teams they need to staff up, and even to determine which agents might need a little more coaching.
  • Knowledge Base and Community: QuiBids forums are used internally to provide detailed information for the team about each market that theyre in, and contact information for their hundreds of vendors – helping the team find the answers they need fast.

Results

Here is what QuiBids is able to achieve using Zendesk:

  • Average Time Saved: 2-3 hours per day
  • First-touch resolution rate: 91%
  • First response time: 14.9 hours, which is 44% faster than the benchmark average for the retail industry at 26.4 hours
  • Tickets per agent: 411

For QuiBids, first response time is everything – it’s an interactive site and fast paced. Customers want to be taken care of quickly since everything is happening real time due to the nature of the site. Without Zendesk, we wouldn’t be able to provide that level of service, noted Josh Walker, Director of Customer Support.
