Customer Stories

Rally.org

Streamlined support for causes and customers

Challenge

Rally.org is an online social fundraising platform that works to empower non-profits, political campaigns, personal causes, and anyone else who wants to raise money online for something they believe in. Rally.org is free and open to all users, who can start a rally page in minutes and immediately begin accepting donations online.

For the first 8-9 months, says Robert Travis, Sales Operations Manager, all the questions that came in were either via email or by phone, and it was basically just a matter of managing an inbox. Still, it wasnt always easy. When Rally.org had several political campaigns happening at the same time, its ticket volume increased by 500%. Says Travis, Transactions shot up in a matter of months. It was unmanageable. Travis knew he needed to find a customer service software solution for Rally.orgs increasing volume. We needed something that was specific to support and managing customers, he says. Something that could track everything, because items were falling through the cracks. Rally.orgs traffic comes via two user segments: donors and fundraisers. Their support team must handle issues ranging from creating a rally page to specific account owner features. About 40-50% of questions are FAQs about billing and refunds. We needed an easy solution, one that would help us manage all of these tickets efficiently – and we needed it fast.

Zendesk as a solution

I researched some other solutions, said Travis, and it came down to Zendesk and a competitor. At the end of the day, Zendesk just made better sense based on what I was seeing in forums and reviews, and from what Id heard through word-of-mouth. Adds Travis, With the free trial, it seemed like there was nothing to lose. The initial setup of Zendesk took Rally.org just a couple of hours. The basic setup was easy, says Travis. You can get up and running in a matter of a few hours and answer questions right away.

Something I love about Zendesk is the integrations, says Travis. Zendesk enables us to use the applications that work best for our business. Its incredibly flexible. Using Zendesk, Rally.org discovered it could manage bugs much more efficiently, too. Zendesk also gives us insight into how things are being addressed, says Travis. Now we can track, manage, and respond to everything much better. Travis continues, It has helped us streamline our customer communications and keep everything in one place.

Rally.org has had such great success with Zendesk, theyve recently rolled it out to their European team, as well. The basic training took less than an hour, says Travis. Its very easy to get new team members up and running.

Heres more about how Rally.org uses Zendesk to deliver customer service thats beautifully simple:

  • With such a high volume of frequently asked questions, the Rally.org team uses macros extensively. Macros are predefined responses which allow rapid replies to these common questions, and enable them to customize responses to more complex inquiries.
  • Zendesks integration with Pivotal Tracker helps the team to track, manage, and respond to bugs much faster. According to Travis, it has been a game changer, allowing them to communicate better with their Engineering group, solve bugs 85% faster, and proactively respond to customers when bugs are fixed.
  • The Rally.org team has created feedback tabs for different pages within their website. This not only allows their end users to ask questions from anywhere, but also gives the team more context about each request. Now they know which page of the site the requester was visiting when they created the ticket!

Providing amazing customer service starts with hiring the right team. But to be successful, you need software that supports not just your customers, but also your agents. Zendesk is easy to set up and easy to use, so my team can focus on whats most important – keeping our donors and fundraisers happy.
