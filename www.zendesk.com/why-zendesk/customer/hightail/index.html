Customer Stories

Hightail

Delivering customer service 35% faster

Challenge

Hightail (formerly YouSendIt), a cloud collaboration company, began 8 years ago as a solution for sending large files without the need for courier services. Today, they have a full product line—including sharing documents, syncing with multiple devices and PCs, and an e-signature featurewhich services 40 million end-users. They needed a customer service software solution that was fast, first and foremost, and they wanted the flow of their agents day-to-day interactions to be smooth.

Our most crucial metric is customer satisfaction, says Mark Stagi, Senior Customer Support Manager. Speed plays a role, obviously, but ultimately we want customers to leave saying that company was awesome with a smile on their faces. Still, Stagi knew his team was not being productive with their previous solution, which was the customer service offering from a popular sales-focused CRM. I knew Zendesk already, says Stagi, and wanted our system to look like Zendesk.

YouSendIt first tried to customize their existing solution to the specifications they wanted, and when that didnt work they hired a 3rd party to try. Over time, it was obvious, says Stagi. I asked myself, Is this the most efficient way to do things? We made the switch to Zendesk and the positive results were immediate.

Zendesk as a solution

I wish there were more tickets for me to do, this is so much more fun! This was the reaction of Amanda, an agent at YouSendIt, after Zendesk was implemented. Stagi says his team immediately enjoyed working in Zendesk, and while it delivered on the goal of faster resolution times and efficiency, those aspects really just contributed to more overall satisfaction—for agents and well as customers.

Our agents receive 60-100 tickets a day per person, Stagi says. And I thought, Is the system were in something we enjoy? If Im using a tool I want it to look good and be easy to use. Zendesk is a much smoother product for us. It makes my team happier. Stagi explains that YouSendIt wanted to increase performance as well as enhance the overall experience for its agents. Zendesk did both. With our previous solution, there was so much that was getting in the way of people being able to take case after case after case in a clean and efficient way, says Stagi. It really was the flow of it. We went from 9 clicks to 2.

Heres more about how YouSendIt uses Zendesk to meet their customer service needs:

  • Macros, or predefined responses, help the YouSendIt team to answer a high volume of frequently asked questions – saving unnecessary work and resolving tickets quickly
  • The team meets every morning to review their reports in Advanced Analytics – giving everyone insight into metrics, such as how many tickets are unanswered in the queue and how their average response time trends day-to-day.
  • Chat makes it easy for agents to access real-time details from customers, enabling them to solve issues faster and increase customer satisfaction.

Results

With Zendesk, YouSendIt has seen the following results for email tickets:

  • Resolution time: Shaved 35% off resolution time per ticket.
  • First response time: 12 hours (47% lower than the industry benchmark)
  • Average resolution time: 15 hours

And, in addition to email, YouSendIt also offers phone-based support and 24/7 live chat support.

We didnt have to do any customization with Zendesk, says Stagi. It was the way we wanted it right out of the box. The setup took less than a day and we started funneling tickets through. The basic rollout was plug-and-play, very smooth, and very straightforward.
