Customer Stories

Resume Companion

Reducing response time across a rapidly growing business

Challenge

Resume Companion, launched in 2009, helps over 400,000 job seekers create resumes each month. With 8 million resumes in their database and over 900,000 registered users, Resume Companion is growing rapidly across 25 countries. With a growing customer base, the company needed a scalable customer service solution that allowed them to provide seamless multi-channel support.

Zendesk as a solution

In looking for a customer service solution, Resume Companion found Zendesk was an easy choice. It was a feature-rich, yet inexpensive option compared to more expensive alternatives. And Resume Companions use of Zendesk has grown organically as their business has grown, utilizing features and integrations such as:

  • Multi-channel support through email, chat, and web portal
  • Custom views for each agent provide managers a peek into performance, and ensures all customers are receiving a world-class customer service experience
  • Macro shortcuts to quickly and efficiently respond to frequently asked questions, leading to greater agent productivity
  • Integration with Flow Dock to enable better communication with software developers

Results

Using Zendesk for resume critiques was a no-brainer – it allowed Resume Companion to keep all communication in one place, the ticket. So, when they began adding customer service agents, extending their use of Zendesk just made sense. Resume Companion has since reduced response time from 3-4 days to under 24 hours, and now can now easily report, monitor and expand their usage to provide fuller, more efficient service to their customers.

By using Zendesk, we have significantly reduced response times and increased efficiency. We were growing so quickly in our startup phase that some customer emails would inevitably slip through the cracks. We needed a solution that could grow with us, and we found that with Zendesk. Now were able to have everything organized in one place, with metrics that allow management to easily keep an eye on quality. We feel like weve just begun to unleash the power of Zendesk, and are looking forward to exploring even more of its features as our business grows.
