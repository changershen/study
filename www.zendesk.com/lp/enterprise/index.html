The Zendesk Difference

Better than easy. It’s beautifully simple.

Zendesk is a modern customer support platform. It’s also a company committed to raising the bar for customer service. As both a product and team, we are focused on helping large companies be human at enterprise scale.

  • People

    The Enterprise team is stacked with talent. They are your partners from the start, using expertise and experience to guide you on the path to success.

  • Product

    Our software is designed to solve problems around speed and efficiency, improve agent productivity, and ultimately build better relationships with customers.

  • Paths

    The enterprise experience is geared toward speed and flexibility. We help you plot the best trajectory and then build a custom path based on your needs.

Meet the team

The Zendesk Enterprise experience is backed up by a hand-picked team of all-stars. They will help you set transformational goals and then make sure you achieve them. This is not an empty promise. It’s an ongoing partnership, committed to proving the value of better customer support.
