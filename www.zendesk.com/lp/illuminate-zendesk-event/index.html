ILLUMINATE

A STRATEGIC APPROACH TO CUSTOMER ENGAGEMENT

San Francisco - 12.4.14
REGISTER New York City - 9.17.14
Watch the Recording

A new era of customer engagement is upon us.

If that sounds a touch dramatic, that’s ok, because we’re trying to make an important point: the voice of the customer has never been louder. Thanks to social media and the changing dynamics of communication, every one of your customers now has the power to bring you more business—or drive it away.

Join us to explore methods for proactively engaging with customers, providing true multi-channel support, and using analytics to drive growth. More importantly, you’ll walk away with actionable advice on building out an engagement model that works for your company.

EVENT AGENDA

2:30PM - 3:00PM: Registration & Networking

3:00PM - 3:30PM: Bringing Customer Service Directly to Your Customers
The voice of the customer has never been louder, which means delivering a modern customer experience and building meaningful relationships is more important than ever. In addition to time and endurance, there are guiding principles you should use to get on the right track and keep you motivated. In this talk, Sam Boonin will discuss the three guiding principles and practical strategies critical to helping companies incite real change.
Sam Boonin, VP Product Marketing, Zendesk
Annie Woo, Vice President, Customer Service, Mindbody
Chris Tabasa, GREE International, Director of Consumer Operations


3:30PM - 4:15PM: Panel Discussion: The Path to Your Customer Engagement Strategy
In this ever-changing world of customer engagement, what exactly do your customers want and expect from you? Do you know which tools and channels are most important to them? Whether you’re building from scratch or evolving, designing a truly effective customer engagement strategy requires exploration of many variables within your business. We’ll discuss the differences, the appropriate paths for a range of business types, and how to be successful on your journey.
Sary Stefanki, Director Customer Success, Zendesk
Mikkel Svane, CEO, Zendesk
Kani Munidasa, Senior Director Engineering - Global Support Services, Pivotal
Bill Lapcevic, VP Customer Success, New Relic


4:15PM - 4:30PM: Break & Demo Stations

4:30PM - 5:15PM: Panel Discussion: The Effortless Experience
Many recent customer experience trends center around making things easier and more convenient for customers. We’ll discuss proactive customer engagement, integration of support directly into customer touchpoints, and using data to increase efficiency and elevate the customer experience. Learn how others have increased customer satisfaction, decreased shopping cart abandonment, and increased conversion. 
Sary Stefanki, Director Customer Success, Zendesk
Marcus Bragg, SVP WW Sales & Success, Zendesk
Diego Nunez, Sr. Director, Digital Marketing & Ecommerce, Skullcandy
Annie Woo, Vice President, Customer Service, Mindbody


5:15 - 5:30 pm: Open Q&A with panelists and audience:
Bring your questions and get ready to voice your opinions as we dive into audience pain points and concerns. This is your opportunity to get opinions or advice from the experts and hear what your community has to say.


5:30 - 6:30 pm: Reception & Demo’s
Follow the conversation and share your thoughts on twitter with #zengage.
