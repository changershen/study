RETAIL IN REAL TIME

What’s in store for the
future of commerce

Until recently, commerce was always connected to a time or place. Now, shopping happens
everywhere, with no restrictions on location, device, or business hours. Stores pop-up in
temporary spaces, purchases are made in-app, and social media fills in the blanks.

For retailers, there’s more to focus on than just service with a smile. What matters is reducing
friction, increasing convenience, and improving the customer experience. And that customer
experience has to be consistent across all brand touchpoints.

Better business comes from better relationships

Zendesk helps manage relationships across all aspects of your business to improve the customer experience. Our platform offers tools that handle everything from phone support to analytics, social media to internal operations. It’s fast and flexible so you can innovate with ease, and scale when you need to grow.

  • Customer Support

    Whether they reach out to you with questions, want to find answers on their own, or need to be proactively engaged, Zendesk makes communicating with customers easy and efficient on any channel — email, web, social, phone, or chat.

  • Vendor Management

    If your supplier has unanswered questions, it can cause delays for your customers. Make it easy for your vendors to work with you. Remove the roadblocks to service by providing them with self-service and assisted support.

  • Employee Satisfaction

    Employees are a lot like internal customers, and treating them as such makes for better relationships. And a good internal help desk—whether for IT or HR—improves communication and helps smooth out operations.
