Why people still love the in-store experience
  •  
  •  
  •  
They enjoy shopping in a clean, organized environment
Shopping online should be just as simple and pleasant
For Example
Gilt
Gilt.com's online store offers a clean, simple experience from browsing to buying to support.
They can find help when they need it
Online shoppers deserve the same
For Example
CB2
Shoppers on CB2.com are often contacted by virtual support rep via an unobtrusive pop-up box. The support rep can help give a more guided experience, but only if the shopper wants.
They can touch the merchandise
Online shoppers still like to explore products from multiple angles
For Example
Zappos
Browsing on Zappos.com mirrors the experience of shopping in-store by letting you view every shoe, in every design, from a variety of angles.
They can get a second opinion
Shoppers want access to feedback and advice
For Example
Modcloth
Shoppers on Modcloth.com have easy access to reviews, ratings, and pictures of other shoppers wearing the clothes they purchased.
Customers like to give things a try
They want to view everything in a real world context
For Example
Ditto
After uploading short videos of themselves, shoppers can virtually try on any of the glasses sold at Ditto.com.
