What’s New in Your Zendesk: Making Customer Service More Effortless

August 18th at 5pm Pacific with Josh Frank, Katherine Kelly, & Steven Yan

When we make support effortless we foster better relationships between businesses and customers. The next “What’s New in Your Zendesk” webinar is focused on new product areas that help to make the customer experience more effortless. Hear from Katherine Kelly and Josh Frank from the Zendesk Product Marketing team, and Steven Yan from the Product Management team – who together will cover the latest feature releases during this 60-minute webinar.

    During this session, we will cover:
    • New channels in Zendesk that enable customers to find help where they already are, such as the Google Play app store and SMS
  • • Product updates to our Web Widget and Help Center that help make customer self-service more frictionless
  • • Improvements to our support and chat tools that will help you make your operations efficient and teams successful
