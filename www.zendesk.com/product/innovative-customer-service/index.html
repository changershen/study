Connect on the right channels

EVERLANE

Everlane talks to their consumers a lot—on social, email, and in-person at Open Studio events. When Facebook’s Businesses on Messenger launched, Everlane saw an opportunity to connect with customers on a new channel. Through an integration with Zopim—a Zendesk company—Everlane customers can now get support and place orders on a new channel they’re familiar with: Messenger.


By providing real-time support and meeting its customers on a new channel, Everlane keeps the conversation open to more conversions.

Get Connected

“At Everlane, we pride ourselves on the personal connection we have with our community,” says Aaron Ginn, Everlane’s Growth Product Manager. “Our close customer relationships are how we differentiate ourselves. Not only can our customers now connect with us through Messenger, but they also can receive personal attention and immediate service from us thanks to Zendesk’s integration.”


- Aaron Ginn, Growth Product Manager

Embed customer service everywhere

LE TOTE

Le Tote makes it effortless for customers to get support in context. By implementing the Zendesk web widget along with Zopim chat, customer support is embedded on every page of Le Tote’s site. When a customer has a question, they can click “Help” and search FAQ articles without interrupting their shopping experience. Or, they can chat with a stylist while browsing.

With Embeddables, Le Tote removed all the barriers between the shopper and the help they're looking for—creating a better end-to-end experience for both the business and customer.

Be everywhere

“Any time they have an issue or if they just want to talk about the tote they received, they can reach out to that person in the company,” says Aubrie Rice, Head of Customer Support. “We also can create triggers in chat, so if a consumer takes a specific action on the site, we can reach out to them. This has been great for helping us automate the personalized experience.”

Design better customer experiences


Bevel uses NPS and CSAT data to drive its support strategy. By listening to customer feedback, the company can better tailor customer service to each customer. Including live one-on-one video tutorials and correspondence methods that match the customer’s preference. Drawing on CSAT and NPS also helps Bevel modernize its product—it has updated several features based on customer requests.


Bevel uses customer feedback to better understand how customers use its products, turning its support team into a product innovation hub.

Provide in-the-moment mobile support

SwiftKey, an app providing smarter prediction technology for easier mobile typing, realized that delivering in-app support was crucial to a seamless user experience. They used the Zendesk Mobile SDK to provide access to all of their Help Center content natively within the app. No web views are required, and a simple integration process makes it easy to deliver a fantastic user experience with minimal hassle.


Swiftkey's approach to providing easy to find, and easy to use self-service will pay off with happier customers and fewer service requests.

It's so easy

Deliver IT to Internal Customers


With over 100 School of Rock franchises across North and South America, it’s clear that this business is doing something right. School of Rock’s fast growth could be due to the innovative way it treats franchisees like customers. School of Rock cares about the franchisee experience. It provides each new franchisee with a suite of best-of-breed tools to support them in opening a new school. Including hands-on support via Zendesk to ensure ongoing help throughout the process.


By crafting great internal customer experiences, School of Rock continues to see its business grow.
